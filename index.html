About

Welcome to my website! My name is Dipika Ghimire and I am a registered nurse with licenses in both Nepal and the United States. I have a passion for helping others and a strong dedication to providing high-quality healthcare. I have a bachelor's degree in nursing from Nobel College and have completed practical training in a variety of healthcare settings, including acute care, long-term care, and home health. I am well-versed in nursing procedures and have experience handling emergency situations. I have extensive knowledge and experience in various areas of healthcare, including working as a staff nurse at Nobel Hospital and serving as a virtual medical assistant in the Children's Endocrinology and Diabetes Centre in Georgia, USA. I am currently also teaching NCLEX to students at NCLEX Preparation Center Nepal, helping to prepare the next generation of healthcare professionals. I am dedicated to staying up-to-date on the latest advances in the field and am always looking for opportunities to further my education and knowledge. I am excited to share my expertise and experience with others and look forward to working with you to achieve your healthcare goals.
